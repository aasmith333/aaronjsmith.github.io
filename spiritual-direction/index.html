Spiritual Direction

Hi. I’m Aaron. I am a poet, a theologian, and a spiritual director. I’ve been deconstructing and reconstructing my faith for over twenty years, and I believe that faith is a process, not a set of certainties. When approaching spiritual direction, I don’t believe I have all the answers to offer, but rather I offer a presence and the ability to hold space for what you are going through as the Spirit does her work in your life. I offer another ear to help hear what God is saying to you, what you are saying to God, and what you are saying to yourself. Hearing the divine through a trauma informed lens allows you to trust yourself, find healing for spiritual wounds, and open space in yourself to trust what you and God are doing. I am fully queer inclusive, love working with people who are deconstructing their faith, and I believe that no matter what your relationship with church is, you have a place at God’s table.
